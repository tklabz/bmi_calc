Privacy Policy for BMI Calculator App

Effective Date: 14th May 2025

Thank you for using the BMI Calculator app. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the information you provide when using our app.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the following information you manually enter in the app:

  • Height
  • Weight

These values are used solely to calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • The data you provide is used immediately and locally on your device to calculate your BMI.
  • We do not store, transmit, or share your height or weight with any third-party services or servers.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • The app does not store any personal data. All calculations happen in real-time on your device and are not saved or logged.
  • We do not collect or retain any information that can identify you personally.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is not specifically directed to children under 13. However, it does not collect any personal or sensitive data from users of any age.

5. Third-party Services

The app does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
